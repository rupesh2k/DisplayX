💡 Make it Shorter (Optional)

Long URLs are hard to type on devices. Create a short link:

  1. Copy your player link above
  2. Go to TinyURL.com or Bit.ly
  3. Paste your long player link
  4. Click "Shorten" to get a short link like: https://tinyurl.com/abc123
  5. Use the short link on your Fire TV, Smart TV, or Raspberry Pi

Example: https://tinyurl.com/352n94m5 → Opens DisplayX player
